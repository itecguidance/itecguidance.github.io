Essential Skills

Interested in pursuing the Software Development concentration? Learn more about the following six essential skills we suggest for a student interested in pursuing the Software Development concentration:


Communication

Students must have strong communications skills to be able to interact and interpret clients need. They must be able to explain the functionality and operation of the application or software.

Teamwork

Students must be able to collaborate with teams in various roles needed for the different stages of the software development life cycle (SDLC). They also must be able to work with team members as they collaborate on multiple software projects.

Analytical Thinking

Students must have strong analytical skills to be able to observe data, break down problems, find solutions, and design software programs.


Creativity

Students must be creative to invent software programs. They also must be creative when planning, designing, and coding their software projects.

Time Management

Students will be working on multiple software projects alongside learning and attending meetings. Students must be able to manage their time in order to be able to deliver all of their projects on time.

Attention to Detail

Students must have strong attention to detail to understand data, to locate bugs within a program, and to design trending software.
