Software Development

Software Development major focuses on the creation, maintainability, and testing of software systems. with the emphasis on team communication. Students will learn Object-oriented programming (OOP) and multiple projects will encourage these learning essentials.

Digital Media

Digital media major introduces students to a life of creativity. with the main focus on creating beautiful animations, videos, and photos. in addition, students will acquire the knowledge of various professional editing tools to help them fulfill their editing goals.

Systems and Security

System and security majors enforce the need for a strong infrastructure. students will be responsible for pinpointing the vulnerability of the system in order to prevent attacks to the network

Enterprise Systems

Enterprise systems major is the Business side of information technology. these roles include organizing and running operations in order to help maintain and manage an organization's IT department Communication and Leadership skills are key to succeed in the field
