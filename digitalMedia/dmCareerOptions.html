Graphic Designer

Career Description:

Graphic Designers create specialized layouts, illustrations, and concepts through computer softwares. They develop overall products and designs for commercial or promotional needs to captivate and inform consumers about products or ideas.

Career Objectives:

  • Incorporate design changes from clients and directors.
  • Use the latest photo, layout, and illustration software.
  • Create visual content, such as company logos, original images, layout for websites, and illustrations to deliver an overall desired message.
  • Review design and concept for errors before publishment.

    • Career Requirements:

  • Be current and up to date with evolving technology with computer software.
  • Knowledge of design techniques by portraying though blueprints, models, and illustrations.
  • Understand media production and communication methods to entertain and inform.

    • Photographer

    Career Description:

    Photographers take photos of landscapes, portraits, merchandise, and subjects by using digital equipment. They may use computer softwares to produce a final product.

    Career Objectives:

  • Use traditional or digital equipment.
  • Use photo editing software to enhance the product quality through specialized effects or manipulation.
  • Set up cameras and equipment.
  • Create artificial lights and adjust camera angles, focus, and shutter speed.

    • Career Requirements:

  • Knowledgeable in the use of electronic equipment and computer software/hardware.
  • Provides Customer Service by attending to the customer’s needs and expectations of quality service to ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Understands the principles of promoting and selling, such as marketing strategies and sale tactics.

    • Film Editor

    Career Description:

    Film Editors create, edit, and manipulate moving images to entertain, inspire, and inform an audience.

    Career Objectives:

  • String, Assemble and organize raw footage together according to scripts and instructions.
  • Trim and reassemble specific clips and scenes to ensure correct timing or presentation.
  • Determine the need for visual effects and audio.

    • Career Requirements:

  • Detail Oriented and Organized
  • Be able to work in a teamwork environment
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills

    • Web Designer

    Career Description:

    Web Designers create, design, and execute content for a website through written and multimedia content.

    Career Objectives:

  • Design and build appealing web content using programming languages,management tools, and digital media.
  • Update website content with new information or media.
  • Create friendly interface design to ensure easy use of navigation.

    • Career Requirements:

  • Prevent violations and abnormalities in the organization's environment.
  • Able to be adaptable and flexible with change in the work environment.
  • Learn and apply up to date technology.

    • 2D/3D Animator

    Career Description:

    Animators use sequential photos of drawings and models that are put together to produce the illusion of moving images, known as Animation.

    Career Objectives:

  • Ability to produce and transform creative ideas into visual content.
  • Create 2D/3D dimension models and images through computer animation and modeling programs.
  • Troubleshoot already implemented security architecture
  • Map out key scenes in storyboarding to ensure the flow of animation.

    • Career Requirements:

  • Collaborates with related departments to ensure the best finalized product.
  • Must be able to meet deadlines within a fast paced environment.

    • Visual Effects Artist

    Career Description:

    Visual Effects Artists are responsible for creating dynamic special effects for films, videos, and other related digital media content.

    Career Objectives:

  • Understand how to place and sequence visual effects.

    • Career Requirements:

  • Collaborates with related departments to ensure the best finalized product.
  • Be familiar with current technological developments in visual/special effects.
  • Able to use VFX software - Maya, After Effects, Nuke, etc.
